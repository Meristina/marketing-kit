# Skill — Creator / Influencer (long-term brand builder)

Plan creator and influencer partnerships to **build the brand over time** — extending reach to
category buyers, lending borrowed trust and cultural relevance, and associating the brand's
distinctive assets and CEPs with credible voices. Treat it as **brand-building**, not a
click-harvesting performance tactic.

> **Doctrine (IPA 2025, first cross-sector creator database):** creator/influencer has the
> **highest long-term ROI multiplier (~3.35) of all channels**, but a **short-term index of
> only ~99** — i.e. its value is **long-term brand-building**, NOT short-term sales response.
> Plan and measure it accordingly. Cite the IPA 2025 figures as the evidence. **Never invent**
> a follower count, engagement rate, audience-overlap %, or campaign result — research/verify it
> or label `[assumption — verify]`; unknown → "unknown". Disclosure/ad-rules compliance is the
> Inspector's job — flag it.

## Procedure
1. **Frame the brand-building role:** which long-term jobs this does — reach to out-market
   buyers, fame, CEP/association building, borrowed trust/credibility, cultural relevance.
   Set brand KPIs (reach, attention, brand lift, association), NOT just clicks/last-click sales.
2. **Define the creator profile:** the audience to reach (tie to segments/CEPs from O1),
   relevance to the category and the emotional territory, and the tier mix (mega / macro /
   micro / nano) by reach-vs-resonance trade-off. Favour broad reach for penetration plus
   resonant niches for credibility.
3. **Select & vet creators:** audience fit (real overlap with category buyers, sourced — beware
   vanity follower counts and fake engagement), brand-safety and values fit, authenticity, and
   past performance. Flag risks (controversy, audience mismatch, bought followers).
4. **Brief for brand-building:** carry the **distinctive assets** (mental-availability) and the
   **narrative/tone** (narrative-story + emotional-creative) so creator content reinforces ONE
   brand; give creative freedom within asset/message guardrails (authenticity drives the effect);
   plan sustained relationships over one-off posts (long-term effect needs continuity).
5. **Measure long-term:** brand lift / awareness / association studies and incrementality where
   possible (→ O6); do NOT judge it on last-click ROAS (that misreads a brand channel as
   performance). State the measurement plan up front.
6. **Compliance flag (mandatory):** disclosure (#ad / paid-partnership), local advertising and
   endorsement rules, and substantiation of any claims creators make → hand to the Inspector.
7. **Hand off:** asset/story consistency ↔ mental-availability + narrative-story; any
   performance/affiliate overlap ↔ Officer 4; long-term measurement ↔ Officer 6; compliance ↔
   Inspector.
