# Skill — Full-Funnel Paid Media

Plan the **paid media** that activates demand now — the channel mix, the budget across funnel
stages, and the measurement — so spend turns into demand without ignoring the brand. This is
the **short-term/activation** half of the 60:40 balance (Officer 4); it runs *alongside* the
long-term brand work (Officer 3), not instead of it.

> **Doctrine — activation that respects the brand.** Plan the funnel end-to-end but keep the
> roles distinct: upper-funnel reach builds demand & carries distinctive assets; lower-funnel
> captures existing intent. **Measure with incrementality first** (geo/holdout, → O6), never
> last-click attribution alone (it over-credits lower-funnel/retargeting — refuted as a sole
> truth). The **overall brand:activation split (~60:40 B2C / ~50/50 B2B) is the budget-balance
> soldier's call (O6)** — here you allocate *within* activation and flight it. **Never invent**
> a CPM, CTR, conversion rate, reach, or ROAS — research/benchmark it or label `[assumption —
> verify]`; unknown → "unknown". Ad-policy/data compliance → Inspector.

## Procedure
1. **Objectives & KPIs per funnel stage:** awareness/reach (reach, CPM, attention) →
   consideration (engagement, video views, site visits) → conversion (CVR, CPA, ROAS) →
   retention/retargeting (repeat, LTV signal). Tie each to the business objective and baseline.
2. **Audience & targeting:** reuse O1 segments/CEPs and the value prop (the promise); define
   prospecting vs retargeting audiences; note privacy-safe targeting (first-party, contextual)
   given signal loss — don't assume deprecated identifiers.
3. **Channel mix by stage & intent:** map candidate channels (e.g. video/CTV, social,
   search, shopping, display/native, audio, OOH, affiliate) to the stage they serve and the
   audience they reach. Justify each by reach × cost × intent fit — not channel fashion.
4. **Budget allocation & flighting:** allocate *within activation* across stages and channels;
   plan flighting/pulsing over time (continuity vs bursts) and creative rotation. State the
   activation total comes from O6's 60:40 split; surface diminishing returns.
5. **Creative & message:** carry the value prop (the promise) and distinctive assets; align
   lower-funnel creative to conversion, upper-funnel to demand/brand. Plan A/B tests.
6. **Measurement plan (mandatory):** incrementality/geo-lift as the truth (→ O6 incrementality),
   MMM for allocation (→ O6 MMM), attribution as a biased diagnostic only — never the sole
   metric. Define holdouts up front.
7. **Hand off:** on-site conversion ↔ conversion-cro; SEO/organic ↔ content-seo; lead handoff ↔
   lead-scoring-lifecycle; the split & causal measurement ↔ O6; brand reach coordination ↔ O3.
